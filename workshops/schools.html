    A Unique Professional Development Program for Modern Educators

    Our AI & IoT Teacher Excellence Program is designed to help teachers seamlessly use AI and hands-on IoT tools to enrich learning, enhance student evaluation, and bring real-world technology into the classroom.

    Developed in accordance with CBSE curriculum frameworks and the NEP 2020 vision, this program transforms teachers into confident, tech-enabled facilitators.

    Why Train Teachers in AI & IoT?

    AI and IoT are no longer optional skills—they are essential components of modern education. Our program helps teachers:

    • Use AI tools to create lesson plans and teaching materials
    • Improve assessment accuracy with AI-driven insights
    • Engage students through practical, hands-on IoT experiments
    • Deliver NEP-aligned experiential and competency-based learning
    • Enhance digital literacy and future-ready skills in students

    KEY PROGRAM FEATURES

    AI for Teaching & Learning

    Teachers learn to use AI to simplify and elevate classroom instruction. Includes:

    • AI-powered lesson planning
    • AI-generated worksheets, quizzes & teaching aids
    • Personalized learning pathways
    • AI for inclusive and differentiated instruction
    • Ethical and responsible AI usage

    AI-Based Student Evaluation

    Transform traditional assessments into smart, insightful evaluations. Teachers learn to:

    • Rubric-based automated marking
    • Analytics dashboards
    • Learning gap identification
    • Personalized feedback reports
    • CBSE competency-based assessment

    IoT Training with Hardware Kits (Grades 3–12)

    Hands-on IoT learning made simple and engaging. What’s included:

    • Junior, Intermediate & Advanced IoT kits
    • Automation & data sensing projects
    • Ready-to-teach lesson plans
    • Teacher training modules

    Classroom Integration Support

    We provide everything teachers need for smooth implementation:

    • CBSE-aligned AI & IoT lesson plans
    • NEP 2020 project templates
    • Assessment tools & rubrics
    • Real-world project guides
